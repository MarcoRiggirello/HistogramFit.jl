Welcome to HistrogramsFit.jl's documentation

The problem of fitting curves to histograms is ubiquitous in High Energy Physics (HEP) and usually it involves three steps:

  1. Determining the "best fit" parameters of a curve;
  2. Determining the errors on the parameters;
  3. Judging the goodness of the fit.

This simple Julia module takes an $nD$ histogram and a data distribution model and creates a theoretically sound chi square statistics that can be used to perform

  1. Point estimation;
  2. Confidence interval estimation;
  3. Goodness-of-fit testing.
